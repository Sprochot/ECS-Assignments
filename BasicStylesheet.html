Major Characters

In Interstellar, Cooper (Matthew McConaughe), is the main character who is tasked with saving the world.

Cooper's daughter Murph (Jessica Chastain), follows in her father's footsteps and becomes the scientist who eventually saves the world.

Brand (Anne Hathaway), is the chief biologist on the mission sent to save the world. She is the first person to settle a new planet.
